Hi, I'm Q. I have a background in architecture and information science, with a deep passion for art and design. This website serves as a showcase for my artwork, which includes Chinese painting, watercolor, pen drawings, and more. Hoping you enjoy exploring my creative journey!

This watercolor painting is my artistic interpretation of a Tang Dynasty lady, inspired by the elegance and opulence of that era. I aimed to capture her poised demeanor and timeless grace through soft brushstrokes and a harmonious blend of colors. The flowing green and red robe, along with her iconic high bun, reflects the richness of Tang Dynasty fashion. I used a muted purple background to create depth and atmosphere, allowing the figure to stand out. The calligraphy inscription adds a historical touch, reinforcing the cultural essence of this piece. Through this work, I express my deep admiration for traditional Chinese art.
